Common Questions

How accurate is the analysis?
Our AI achieves 90%+ accuracy for most popular models like Midjourney, DALL-E, and Stable Diffusion. Accuracy varies based on image quality and how heavily the image was edited.
Is my uploaded image stored?
No. All processing happens in your browser. We never store, save, or transmit your images to any server. Your privacy is our priority.
Is it really free?
Yes! The core analyzer is completely free with no limits. We may add premium features in the future, but basic analysis will always be free.
Can I analyze video?
Currently we support images only. Video analysis is on our roadmap and coming soon!
