Your Privacy Matters

Overview

PromptVision AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

  • Images: Images you upload are processed locally in your browser and are NOT transmitted to our servers.
  • Local Storage: We store your preferences (theme, history) in your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device.
  • API Keys: If you provide an API key, it is stored locally in your browser and used only to communicate with the AI service you've chosen.

How We Use Information

  • To provide image analysis functionality
  • To remember your preferences and history
  • To improve our service based on anonymous usage patterns

Information Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. The only exception is when you use an external AI API (like Gemini), your image data is sent to that service according to their privacy policy.

Cookies & Tracking

PromptVision does NOT use cookies, tracking pixels, or any analytics tools that collect personal data. Your browsing is completely private.

Data Security

Since all processing happens locally in your browser, your data is as secure as your own device. We recommend:

  • Using a secure, updated browser
  • Not sharing your device with untrusted users
  • Clearing browser history if using a shared computer

Children's Privacy

PromptVision is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.
