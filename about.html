About Me

Hi! I'm Hallu Dodamani, a passionate Full Stack Developer from India. I love building tools that solve real-world problems and make people's work easier.

I created PromptVision after noticing how many creators struggle to understand how amazing AI artwork was made. Whether you see a stunning Midjourney image on Twitter or a beautiful DALL-E creation on Reddit, the question is always the same: "What prompt did they use?"

That's when I decided to build a solution that uses AI to reverse-engineer the prompts behind any AI-generated image. Now, with PromptVision, you can discover the exact prompts, models, and settings used to create any AI masterpiece.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or sharing knowledge with the developer community.
